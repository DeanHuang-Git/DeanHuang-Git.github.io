The goal is to make three meaningful interactive visualizations for the Malaria Dataset collected by TidyTuesday

1st Visualization: What is the death rate per 100,000 in Africa?

  - The interactive visualization was plotted using choropleth from plotly.

  - Make sure to have country_code as your location, death_rate_per_100,000 as your color, year as your animation frame, and africa as your scope.

2nd Visualization: What are the incidents of malaria per 1000 for different countries (2000-2015)?

 - The interactive visualization was plotted using choropleth from plotly.

 - Make sure to have country_code as your location, malaria_incidents_per_1,000 as your color, year as your animation frame, and world as your scope.

3rd Visualization: What is the mean total deaths by age group (1990-2016)?

 - The interactive visualization was plotted using line from plotly.

 - Before plotting, make sure to group by year and age group then calculate the mean.

 - Make sure to have year as x, total deaths as y, and age group as color.
